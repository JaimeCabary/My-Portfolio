About Me

Hi, I'm Shalom Chidi-Azuwike, a 20-year-old software engineering student at FUTO with a CGPA of 4.86. I am passionate about innovation, storytelling, and making a positive impact in my community.

My love for reading, which began at age four, ignited my journey as a writer. I've authored books like The Billionaire's Son and Caught in Between under the pseudonym Jaime Cabary.

I've developed three websites, including my portfolio, and designed an app called Bizvest. I'm a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador and Samsung Student Ambassador, advocating for digital skills and innovation.

My goal is to create robotic works of art to better humanity. Inspired by visionaries like Elon Musk, I aspire to visit Mars and contribute to making humanity a multiplanetary species.
